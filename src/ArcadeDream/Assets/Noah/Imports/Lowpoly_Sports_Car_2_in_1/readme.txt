Well optimized model of a sports car that comes in two variants, regular and race. 

This is a very good looking low-poly asset, package includes models of cars with separate wheels, textures & materials, prefabs for both models and a scene file. 

Regular model - 1326 triangles

Race model - 1500 tiangles

Textures include diffuse, normal and specular maps.
